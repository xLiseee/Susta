OUR SERVICES

What We Offer

Sustainability

We provide complete sustainability solutions, integrating SDGs, GRI, and Sustatool. Aligning objectives with strategic goals, we ensure CSRD and EU-Taxonomy compliance. Our services include CO2 footprint calculation, reduction plans, and Ecovadis audit support.

Growth

We optimize value proposition and strategy, emphasizing excellence, customer focus, and innovation. Our approach includes diversification and innovative models like as-a-service and circularity. We address risks through ERM, guiding organizational redesign for growth.

People & organization

Crafting a holistic HR policy, aligning with company values. Providing tailored coaching for teams and executives, focusing on culture and behavior. Enhancing interdepartmental collaboration and team dynamics. Implementing growth plans and establishing an internal training center.
